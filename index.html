About me

Hi, my name is Nazarine Wasonga, and I was born the 7th of December, 1998. I'm from Nairobi. I have graduated from high school the previous year. The course I took was Information Technology. Since October 2020 I have enthusiastically been studying programming, not only in school, but also via Internet. I think I have tried every possible way of learning, as in, watching many hours of video tutorials, learning through websites like freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, and many others, and also learning through the mobile app Sololearn. I also finished two courses, eSova (JavaScript) and Clever (HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript). The last couple of months I've mostly been reading e-books about programming. As a result, I now have enough knowledge to make programs using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, C#, T-SQL, ASP.NET MVC, Git. I'm currently looking to get a job as a junior programmer using the skills I already possess. I'm a workholic and I see myself as a future programmer. I love creating stuff with code, it's like having superpowers in real life, and yes, in my younger years I read many comics.
